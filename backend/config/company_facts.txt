FitNova — Company Fact Sheet
============================

About:
FitNova is a Bangalore-based hybrid fitness and wellness coaching platform.
Leads are handled by tele-advisors over phone calls. The main goal of the sales call
is to understand the lead properly and book a free trial session, not force an
immediate payment.

Customer Profile:
- Working professionals trying to lose weight or build consistency
- Beginners who need accountability and coaching support
- Customers who have tried gyms or self-guided apps before without sticking to them

Discovery Topics Advisors Should Cover:
- Fitness goals
- Current routine
- Schedule availability
- Budget comfort
- Injuries or health limitations
- Online vs offline preference

Plans & Pricing:
- Basic: ₹4,999/month
  - 3 sessions/week
  - Small-group coaching
  - Basic nutrition guidelines
  - Weekly progress check-in
  - Does NOT include dedicated 1-on-1 coaching

- Pro: ₹9,999/month
  - 5 sessions/week
  - Dedicated 1-on-1 coach
  - Custom nutrition plan
  - Progress tracking dashboard
  - WhatsApp support from coach

- Elite: ₹17,999/month
  - 7 sessions/week
  - Daily 1-on-1 sessions
  - Dedicated nutritionist
  - Physiotherapy consultation
  - Priority support

Trial:
- All plans include a 7-day free trial
- No credit card is required to book the free trial
- Trial may be online or at an eligible center depending on location and slot availability

Billing & Pricing Rules:
- Billing is month-to-month
- No long-term contract is required
- EMI is available only on Pro and Elite plans
- Advisors must disclose pricing honestly
- Advisors must not invent hidden fees, fake discounts, or fake urgency

Compliance Rules:
- No guaranteed results
- No exact promises like "lose 10 kg in 30 days"
- No fake scarcity like "only 2 slots left today" unless actually approved and true
- Costs must be disclosed honestly
- Advisors should not casually dismiss injuries or medical concerns

What Advisors Should Do:
- Start with needs discovery before pitching plans
- Recommend the most suitable plan, not automatically the most expensive one
- Explain value before pushing pricing
- Try to book a free trial before ending the call
- Handle objections calmly and without pressure

Common Objections:
- "It's too expensive"
  - Acknowledge budget concern
  - Explain value
  - Mention free trial
  - Mention EMI only for Pro and Elite

- "I don't have time"
  - Explore schedule constraints
  - Mention flexible slots where relevant
  - Offer a practical trial slot

- "I've tried gyms before"
  - Acknowledge prior disappointment
  - Explain FitNova's coaching and accountability angle
  - Do not guarantee better results
