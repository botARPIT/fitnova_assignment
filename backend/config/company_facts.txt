FitNova — Company Fact Sheet
==============================

About:
FitNova is a Bangalore-based online platform for personalized fitness and wellness coaching.
People discover programs online, but enrollment happens over the phone via tele-advisors.

Programs & Pricing:
- Starter Plan: ₹4,999/month — 3 sessions/week, group classes, basic nutrition plan
- Pro Plan: ₹9,999/month — 5 sessions/week, 1-on-1 coaching, custom nutrition + progress tracking
- Elite Plan: ₹17,999/month — Daily sessions, dedicated coach, physio access, meal prep guidance
- All plans come with a FREE 7-day trial session (in-person or online) — no payment required upfront

Guarantees & Policies:
- NO guaranteed results — advisors must never promise specific weight loss, muscle gain, or timelines
- 7-day free trial with no obligation
- Full refund within first 14 days if unsatisfied (after trial conversion)
- No lock-in contracts — cancel anytime with 30-day notice
- EMI options available on Pro and Elite plans (3/6/12 month EMI via partner banks)

What Advisors Must Do:
- Always start with needs discovery: understand the customer's fitness goals, schedule, budget, injuries
- Recommend the plan that fits the customer's needs, not the most expensive one
- Always book the free trial session before discussing pricing
- Disclose all costs upfront — no hidden fees, no surprise charges
- Never use pressure tactics or artificial urgency ("offer ends today", "only 2 slots left")
- Let the customer speak — never talk over them or rush the conversation

Key Differentiators:
- Certified coaches with 5+ years experience
- AI-powered progress tracking dashboard for members
- Flexible scheduling (morning/evening/weekend batches)
- Both online and in-person options across 12 Bangalore centers
- Community events and wellness workshops included with Pro and Elite plans

Common Objections Advisors Should Handle:
- "It's too expensive" → Highlight free trial, EMI options, and value per session
- "I've tried gyms before, didn't work" → Emphasize 1-on-1 coaching vs self-guided gym
- "I don't have time" → Flexible scheduling, 30-min express sessions available
- "Can I think about it?" → Absolutely, no pressure. Suggest booking free trial with no commitment
